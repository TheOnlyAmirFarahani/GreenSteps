Welcome to GreenSteps

Small Steps, Big Impact

GreenSteps is designed to help you make more sustainable travel choices while keeping track of your health. By logging your daily movements, you can see how much carbon emissions you’ve avoided and how many calories you’ve burned—turning everyday actions into meaningful progress for both you and the planet.

Why Choose GreenSteps?

  • Measure Your Impact: Track how much CO₂ you save by walking, cycling, or using public transport.
  • Stay Active: See the calories you burn as you move through your day.
  • Set Goals & Stay Motivated: Earn achievement badges and monitor your progress over time.
  • Learn & Improve: Get practical tips on reducing your carbon footprint.

How It Works

  1. Log Your Travel: Enter your mode of transportation, distance, and time.
  2. Get Instant Insights: See real-time data on emissions saved and calories burned.
  3. Track Your Progress: Review your weekly and monthly statistics in a user-friendly dashboard.
  4. Stay Inspired: Earn rewards and compare your impact over time.

Join the Movement

Every step you take brings us closer to a healthier planet. Start tracking your journey with GreenSteps today!
