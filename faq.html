Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is GreenSteps? +

    GreenSteps is a web app designed to help users adopt environmentally friendly travel routines by tracking the carbon emissions they avoid and the calories they burn by walking.

  • How does GreenSteps work? +

    Users input their travel details, and the app calculates the emissions they have avoided by walking instead of using other transportation methods. It also estimates the calories burned based on distance traveled.

  • How do I log my travel details? +

    You can log your travel details by filling out a simple form on the Ecotracker.

  • How can I learn more about Global Warming? +

    Visit the Global Warming page to learn more!

  • How often is my data updated? +

    Your travel log updates whenever you enter new data.

  • What types of achievements can I earn? +

    GreenSteps offers various achievements based on milestones, such as:

    • Walking 10,000 steps
    • Avoiding 10 kg of CO₂ emissions
  • Do achievements reset over time? +

    No, your achievements are saved and tracked as long as you use the app.

  • Can I switch between dark mode and light mode? +

    Yes! GreenSteps supports both dark and light modes. You can switch between them for better readability.

  • Is my data secure? +

    Absolutely! GreenSteps will not share your data with anyone.

  • Who do I contact for support? +

    If you need help or have any questions, visit the Contact Us page for more details.

  • How do I report a bug? +

    If you encounter any issues, visit the Contact Us page to report a bug. We appreciate your feedback.
