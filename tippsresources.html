Tips

  • Start off small by just walking short distances and gradually increase the distance.
  • Give yourself daily goals and then try to achieve them every day.
  • Incorporate walking into your daily routine, such as walking to work or taking the stairs instead of the elevator.
  • Stay hydrated during your walks to prevent fatigue and maintain energy.
  • Dress appropriately for your current weather conditions.
  • Wear proper shoes to make it comfortable for your feet.
  • Listen to music as you walk to pass the time.
  • Try to make a routine by walking at the same time daily to get your body adjusted.
  • Walk with your head up and shoulders relaxed.
